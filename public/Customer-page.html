Company Overview

Founded in 2019, HTS aims to provide excellent service in transport with the view
to perform the role of service to the logistics department and complete customer
service for transport solution. In this highly customer oriental world, where the
concerned customer wants the best, “We extend value for your time”.
We served well equipped fleet of vehicle for your need, your growth and satisfaction.
We are committed to continuous improvement in Logistics and offer superior quality
services standard to customer.
HILLSTER TRUCKING has been able to secure all relevant licenses and permits to operate
throughout the Philippines. We will ensure that we abide by the rules and regulations
of the trucking industry and we will only hire experienced and qualified drivers with
valid commercial driver’s license (CDL).

Vision and Mission

Company History

The business name was derived from the combination of the words 'HILL' and 'SISTER'.
HILL literally means hill, which pertains to the address of this business, 'Burol'.
While on the other side, 'SISTER' pertains to the two daughters of the owner.
